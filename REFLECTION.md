# Reflection

## Were your predictions accurate?

My predictions were mostly accurate. Creative activities had some of the highest enjoyment scores, and creative was also the category that appeared the most during my week.

## What surprised you about your week?

I was surprised that shorter activities sometimes had the highest enjoyment ratings. This showed that an activity does not need to take a long time to be meaningful.

## Which higher-order function was most useful and why?

The most useful higher-order function was filter() because it helped me quickly find activities that matched specific conditions. It made analyzing my weekly data easier without needing complicated loops.
